    What are some things to keep in mind when adopting a pet?
  • Financial costs (pet food, grooming, obedience training, pet supplies, veterinary care), Pet allergies, Landlord approval, Time commitment


    • What are some tips for first time pet owners?
  • Prep the home and members of the home. It is also beneficial to help your pet adapt to your home and daily routines. Know your capacity to care for a pet and the activity that comes with adopting a new pet. Bonding time is important in developing a strong relationship with your pet. Patience is key when first adopting and training a pet!
